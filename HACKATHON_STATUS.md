## üì± Demo Flow (5 minutes)

### Act 1: Setup (1 min)
1. Open app at `http://localhost:3000`
2. Enter location: "Da Nang, Vietnam"
3. Select "CITY ODYSSEY" campaign type

### Act 2: Quest Generation (30 sec)
1. Watch loading animation
2. AI generates 4-5 culturally-relevant quests

### Act 3: Navigation (1 min)
1. Select first quest
2. Click "VIEW AREA ON MAP" ‚Üí Shows fuzzy location
3. Demonstrate GPS tracking starting
4. Show live journey stats card

### Act 4: Verification (1.5 min)
1. Click "SCAN LOCATION"
2. Take photo (or upload sample)
3. AI analyzes and provides feedback
4. **Demo success case:** Quest verified ‚úì
5. **Demo failure case:** Quest rejected ‚úó
6. Click "APPEAL TO AI JUDGE"
7. Provide context: "The station is green, not blue"
8. Show GPS distance indicator
9. Submit appeal ‚Üí AI reconsiders ‚Üí Success!

### Act 5: Completion (1 min)
1. Complete final quest
2. Journey completion screen appears
3. Show stats: distance traveled, duration, waypoints

5. Opens native Maps app with walking directions
